Design Decisions

  • Grouped by category — Facility, Utilities, Insurance, Administrative, Marketing (all customizable)
  • Inline editing — amounts are editable right in the list, no modal needed
  • "+ Add Category" button creates new groups or items within groups
  • Three-dot menu (⋮) — rename, move to different group, delete
  • Instructor pay excluded — it's auto-calculated from class data, shown as a note
  • Running total at the bottom so the owner always knows where they stand
